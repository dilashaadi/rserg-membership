#About
The Renewable and Sustainable Energy Resource Group (RSERG) is a student-led academic network initiated in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Kathmandu University. The group is aiming to bring together students, scholars and professionals from all around the world with a strong interest in renewable and sustainable energy systems to engage in learning, research, and practical initiatives.

RSERG works to promote clean energy awareness, innovation, and interdisciplinary knowledge exchange through workshops, seminars, training programs, and community-oriented outreach.

Promoting Energy Awareness and Literacy
Building Technical and Leadership Skills
Fostering Innovation and Research Culture
Supporting Community-Based Energy Initiatives
Creating a Network of Energy Enthusiasts and Professionals
